Feneryss

Feneryss is the name of both a continent and the world as a whole: the continent is the entirety of the known world and is believed to be the only habitable land that remains in the wake of the Reckoning. It is encircled by The Maw, a sea of molten magma. All that have attempted to fly beyond the shores of Feneryss in search of other land have returned emptyhanded-- or never returned at all. Humanity has arrayed itself around a handful of city-states: Pulse - a city within an ever-expanding wall; Lanis - the massive remains of a failed rocket; Lokoran - the last pure cistern in the known world; Sylvan Hollow - a sunken pre-Reckoning city; Neroth - a chaotic frontier settlement; and Hel - the origin point of the Undead Tide and home of an eclectic Vampire Coven. Together, these city-states account for 95% of all living people, the remainder scattered throughout settlements in between.
